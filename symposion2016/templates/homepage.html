How to Submit your Proposal

  1. Sign up for an account: https://cfp.pycon.ca/account/signup/.
  2. Click the confirmation link in your email
  3. Sign in again: https://cfp.pycon.ca/account/login/.
  4. Click the "Dashboard" link: https://cfp.pycon.ca/dashboard/.
  5. Click "Create speaker profile" link: https://cfp.pycon.ca/speaker/create/.
  6. Click "Submit a new proposal" then the "Talk/Tutorial" link: https://cfp.pycon.ca/proposals/submit/talk/.
  7. Fill out that form, click “Save” (or “Save and Add Additional Speakers” if someone else will be giving this talk with you) and you’re all done!
